Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to create an account?
Nope! Just click "Find Opponent" and start debating. No signup required.
What if I get matched with a troll?
Click "Next Opponent" to instantly find someone new. We also have a report system for abusive users.
Can I choose my topic?
Topics are random to keep things interesting! But don't worry, we have 100+ controversial topics so you'll never be bored.
How does spectator voting work?
Spectators vote during the open debate (30s). Results are shown live so you can see who's winning!
Is this free?
Yes! Tug O Word is completely free. No hidden fees, no premium tiers.
What devices work?
Works on any device with a camera and mic - desktop, laptop, phone, or tablet. Best on Chrome or Safari.
