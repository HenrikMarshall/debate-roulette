Community Guidelines

How to debate like a civilized human

Our Philosophy: Debate ideas, not people. Argue passionately, but respectfully. We encourage hot takes and controversial opinions, but not harassment or hate.

The Golden Rules

✅ DO

  • Argue your position passionately
  • Challenge ideas and arguments
  • Use facts and logic
  • Admit when you're wrong
  • Have fun and be creative
  • Use emojis for reactions
  • Vote fairly as a spectator

❌ DON'T

  • Attack people personally
  • Use slurs or hate speech
  • Harass or threaten anyone
  • Share explicit content
  • Spam or advertise
  • Dox or share private info
  • Record others without consent

Debate Etiquette

  • Listen: When your opponent is speaking, actually listen. You'll debate better.
  • Stay on topic: Respond to the actual debate topic, not random tangents.
  • No shouting: Raised voices ≠ better arguments. Stay calm.
  • Time limits: Respect the 30-second time limits. Don't talk over your time.
  • Concede gracefully: If you lose, accept it. There's always another debate.

Prohibited Content

The following will result in immediate removal and possible ban:

  • Hate speech: Racism, sexism, homophobia, transphobia, religious hatred
  • Violence: Threats, glorification of violence, graphic content
  • Sexual content: Nudity, sexual acts, sexual harassment
  • Illegal activity: Promotion of illegal drugs, weapons, hacking, etc.
  • Self-harm: Promotion or glorification of suicide or self-injury
  • Minors: Any sexualization of minors
  • Doxxing: Sharing someone's private information
  • Spam: Advertising, MLM schemes, repetitive content

Spectator Guidelines

  • Chat respectfully: No harassment in spectator chat
  • Vote honestly: Vote for who actually debated better, not based on looks/identity
  • Don't spoil: If you recognize someone, keep it to yourself
  • Be entertaining: Good commentary makes debates better

What Happens If You Break Rules?

Depending on severity:

  • Warning: First offense for minor violations
  • Temporary ban: 24 hours to 7 days
  • Permanent ban: Serious violations or repeat offenders
  • Report to authorities: Illegal content or credible threats

How to Report Violations

If you encounter rule violations:

  • Click "Next Opponent" to immediately leave
  • Use the Report button (coming soon)
  • Email serious issues to: report@tugoword.com

Controversial Topics ≠ Hate

Important distinction:

✅ "I think immigration policies should be stricter" = OK

❌ "All immigrants are criminals" = NOT OK


✅ "Traditional marriage has benefits" = OK

❌ "Gay people are going to hell" = NOT OK


Debate the policy, not the people.

Tips for Better Debates

  • Steel-man, don't straw-man: Address the strongest version of their argument
  • Use examples: "For instance..." makes arguments concrete
  • Ask questions: "How would that work?" exposes weak arguments
  • Know when to pivot: If losing one point, shift to stronger ground
  • End strong: Your last 5 seconds matter most

The Spirit of Tug O Word

We built this platform because civil discourse is dying. Social media rewards outrage, not reasoning. We want to bring back the art of debate - where you can:

  • Disagree without being disagreeable
  • Challenge ideas without attacking people
  • Change your mind when presented with good arguments
  • Have fun arguing about things that don't really matter

Remember: The best debates happen when both sides argue in good faith. Be the debater you'd want to face.
