Data Deletion Request

Request deletion of your Hot Take account and data

What gets deleted:
  • Your account information (name, email, profile photo)
  • Debate history and statistics
  • All associated user data

Note: This action is permanent and cannot be undone. Deletion is completed within 30 days.

Automatic Deletion (Recommended)

The easiest way to delete your data:

  1. Go to your Facebook Apps and Websites settings
  2. Find "Hot Take" in the list
  3. Click "Remove"
  4. Your data will be automatically deleted within 30 days

Manual Deletion Request

Alternatively, submit a deletion request below:

✓ Your deletion request has been submitted. We'll process it within 30 days and send a confirmation email.

What Happens Next?

  1. Immediate: Your account is marked for deletion
  2. Within 7 days: You'll receive a confirmation email
  3. Within 30 days: All your data is permanently deleted
  4. Completion: You'll receive a final confirmation

Data Retention

After deletion, we may retain certain information for legitimate business purposes:

  • Legal compliance (if required by law)
  • Fraud prevention (anonymized)
  • Analytics (aggregated, non-identifiable)
